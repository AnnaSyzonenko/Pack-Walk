Before you start walking
PackWalk connects strangers. Here's how to stay safe and have a great experience.
📍
Always meet in public — parks, café entrances, squares. Never at someone's home for a first meeting.
👁️
Check the profile first — review photos, social links, ratings, and verified badge before going.
🐕
Dog etiquette — let dogs sniff from a distance first. Ask before letting them greet. Keep leash on until both dogs are calm.
💬
Chat before meeting — exchange a few messages first. Any hesitation — trust your gut.
🚨
If something feels wrong — leave calmly. Use the Report button. Your safety always comes first.
📱
Tell someone where you're going — share your meetup spot before heading out, especially for a first meeting.

🛡️ Safety Guide

📍
Always choose public meetup spots
Meet at the marked spots — parks, café entrances, squares. Never at someone's home for the first meeting.
👁️
Check the profile before going
Review social links, photos, and past walk ratings. Verified users have a blue badge — prioritise them.
🐕
Dog interaction etiquette
Let dogs sniff from a distance first. Keep the leash until both dogs are clearly comfortable. Ask before letting them greet.
💬
Chat before meeting
Exchange a few messages first. Any hesitation — trust your gut and reschedule.
🚨
If something feels wrong
Leave calmly. Use the "Report" button on any profile. Your safety always comes first.
📱
Tell someone where you're going
Share your meetup spot with a friend or family member before heading out, especially for a first meeting.
