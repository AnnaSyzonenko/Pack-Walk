🛡️ Safety Guide

📍
Always choose public meetup spots
Meet at the marked spots — parks, café entrances, squares. Never at someone's home for the first meeting.
👁️
Check the profile before going
Review social links, photos, and past walk ratings. Verified users have a blue badge — prioritise them.
🐕
Dog interaction etiquette
Let dogs sniff from a distance first. Keep the leash until both dogs are clearly comfortable.
💬
Chat before meeting
Exchange a few messages first. Any hesitation — trust your gut and reschedule.
🚨
If something feels wrong
Leave calmly. Use the "Report" button on any profile. Your safety always comes first.
